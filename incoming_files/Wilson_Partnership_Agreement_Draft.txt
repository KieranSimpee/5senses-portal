PREAMBLE

This agreement is a recognition of trust, shared vision, and mutual commitment between the Founder and the Partner.

The Founder acknowledges that the Partner has demonstrated dedication, capability, and strong execution during the initial trial period commencing February 2026. This agreement is not merely a business arrangement — it is a statement of appreciation and a promise of a shared future.

The Founder believes that every person in SIMPLEX-ITY has the right to define who they want to be within the company. No role is imposed. Identity is chosen. This agreement reflects that philosophy.

---

1. PURPOSE OF THIS AGREEMENT

1.1 This agreement formalises the ongoing partnership between the Founder and the Partner in the building, development, and growth of the SIMPLEX-ITY platform.

1.2 It is the intention of both parties to build a self-sustaining platform business together, with the Partner playing a central and meaningful role in its success.

1.3 This agreement supersedes any prior verbal or informal arrangements between both parties.

---

2. ROLE & RESPONSIBILITIES

2.1 The Partner shall take ownership of and be responsible for the areas of the business mutually agreed upon between both parties from time to time.

2.2 The Partner is encouraged to define their own identity, role, and contribution within the company. No title is imposed. The Partner has full freedom to choose how they wish to be known within SIMPLEX-ITY.

2.3 Both parties agree to communicate openly and regularly regarding priorities, direction, and expectations.

---

3. EQUITY & PROFIT PARTICIPATION

3.1 The Founder recognises the significant contribution of the Partner and wishes to offer a meaningful stake in the future success of SIMPLEX-ITY.

3.2 The specific equity percentage to be granted to the Partner is TO BE CONFIRMED (TBC) by mutual agreement between both parties. This will be documented in a formal addendum to this agreement once finalised.

3.3 The equity structure shall be structured as Phantom Equity — meaning the Partner receives the financial benefit equivalent to an equity stake in the company, including a proportionate share of any future profits, distributions, or exit proceeds, without holding formal voting shares in 5SENSESBEAUTY LIMITED.

3.4 The equity percentage shall be tied to the growth and performance of the SIMPLEX-ITY platform and may increase over time based on milestones mutually agreed by both parties.

3.5 At all times and under all circumstances, the ultimate and final ownership of SIMPLEX-ITY and 5SENSESBEAUTY LIMITED shall remain with KIERAN LI (LI, Chi Nok) as the sole Founder and legal owner.

---

4. VESTING & GROWTH MILESTONES

4.1 The Partner's phantom equity shall vest progressively based on continued active contribution to the company.

4.2 Specific vesting milestones and performance targets shall be agreed upon by both parties and documented in a separate Milestone Addendum.

4.3 Both parties acknowledge that the equity percentage may grow as the company grows, rewarding long-term commitment and platform success.

---

5. TERMINATION

5.1 This agreement shall NOT be terminated by either party unilaterally.

5.2 Any termination, modification, or dissolution of this agreement requires the written consent and agreement of BOTH parties — the Founder and the Partner.

5.3 In the event of a mutual agreement to terminate, the terms of any outstanding phantom equity entitlements shall be settled fairly and documented in writing before termination takes effect.

5.4 This clause exists to protect both parties equally and to reflect the trust and commitment that this partnership represents.

---

6. CONFIDENTIALITY

6.1 Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality regarding all business operations, strategies, client relationships, financial information, and platform data of SIMPLEX-ITY.

6.2 This confidentiality obligation shall survive the termination of this agreement.

---

7. GOVERNING LAW

7.1 This agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Hong Kong SAR.

7.2 Any disputes arising from this agreement shall first be resolved through good faith discussion between both parties before any formal legal action is considered.

---

8. SPIRIT OF THIS AGREEMENT

8.1 This agreement is written in the spirit of trust, appreciation, and shared ambition.

8.2 The Founder wishes Wilson to know that there is a real and meaningful future in this company for those who commit to building it.

8.3 Both parties enter this agreement not as employer and employee, but as two people who believe in the same vision and are committed to making it real together.
