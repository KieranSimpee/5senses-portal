PREAMBLE / 序言

This agreement upgrades the relationship between the Founder and the Partner from an initial trial period (Months 1–3) into a formal structured partnership commencing Month 4 (June 2026).

本協議將創辦人與合夥人的關係由初始試驗期（第1至3個月）正式升級為結構化合夥關係，由第4個月（2026年6月）起生效。

The Founder has borne all development and operational costs during the first three months. The Partner has contributed time, skills, and execution capacity. This agreement now formalises the next phase, where equity participation is earned through defined operational milestones, government funding success, and sustained commitment.

創辦人於首三個月承擔所有開發及營運費用。合夥人以時間、技能及執行能力作出貢獻。本協議正式確立下一階段安排，股權參與須透過達成既定營運里程碑、政府資助申請成功及持續承諾來獲取。

4. EQUITY PARTICIPATION STRUCTURE / 股權參與架構

IMPORTANT NOTE ON STRUCTURE / 架構重要說明:
Equity participation shall initially be structured as Phantom Equity — financial participation rights equivalent to a shareholding, without formal share issuance. Formal share transfer into 5SENSESBEAUTY LIMITED shall occur upon mutual agreement at specific milestones as defined below. This protects the Founder's legal ownership during the early phase while honouring the Partner's earned participation.

股權參與初期以虛擬股權形式架構——即相當於持股的財務參與權利，暫不正式發行股份。正式股份轉讓至5SENSESBEAUTY LIMITED將於雙方同意及達成以下特定里程碑後進行。此安排在早期階段保護創辦人的法律所有權，同時承認合夥人所獲取的參與權利。

5.1 The Partner acknowledges that securing government funding is a core KPI of this partnership.
合夥人確認爭取政府資助是本合夥關係的核心關鍵績效指標。

所獲政府資助將按雙方同意用於公司營運開支。未經創辦人書面同意，合夥人不得挪用、濫用或將資助用於批准範圍以外的用途。

5.4 Any government funding obtained shall first cover operational costs. Surplus funds shall be reinvested into the company unless otherwise agreed in writing.
所獲政府資助應首先用於支付營運費用。除非另有書面協議，盈餘資金應再投入公司。

6.1 Phantom Equity Phase (Months 4–6):

No formal shares issued. The Founder issues a written letter confirming the Partner's entitlement to 3% phantom equity, which converts to formal shares upon Tranche 2 conditions being met.
不發行正式股份。創辦人發出書面信函，確認合夥人享有3%虛擬股權權益，於第二批條件達成後轉換為正式股份。

6.2 First Formal Share Transfer (Month 12):
首次正式股份轉讓（第12個月）：
Upon Tranche 2 conditions being met, the Founder shall instruct the Company Secretary to prepare a share transfer form. The Partner's name shall be formally registered as a 4% shareholder in 5SENSESBEAUTY LIMITED. All associated stamp duty and legal fees shall be borne by the company.
於第二批條件達成後，創辦人應指示公司秘書準備股份轉讓表格。合夥人名字將正式登記為5SENSESBEAUTY LIMITED的4%股東。所有相關印花稅及法律費用由公司承擔。

6.3 Second Formal Share Transfer (Month 18):
第二次正式股份轉讓（第18個月）：
Upon Tranche 3 conditions being met, a further share transfer shall be executed, increasing the Partner's registered shareholding to 12% in total.
於第三批條件達成後，將執行進一步股份轉讓，將合夥人的登記持股總額增至12%。

6.4 All share transfers shall be conducted in accordance with the Companies Ordinance (Cap. 622) of Hong Kong and shall be properly documented with the Companies Registry.
所有股份轉讓均須按照香港《公司條例》（第622章）進行，並須在公司註冊處妥善記錄。

7. TERMINATION & EXIT / 終止及退出

7.1 Either party may terminate this agreement by providing 30 calendar days' written notice.
任何一方可提供30個日曆天書面通知終止本協議。

7.2 Upon termination by the Partner:
合夥人終止時：
(a) All unvested phantom equity and unearned tranches are immediately cancelled.
所有未歸屬虛擬股權及未獲取批次即時取消。
(b) Formally transferred shares (vested tranches) are retained by the Partner but are subject to a Right of First Refusal — the Founder has the right to purchase the Partner's shares at fair market value before any third-party sale.
正式轉讓股份（已歸屬批次）由合夥人保留，但須受優先購買權約束——在任何第三方出售前，創辦人有權以公平市值購買合夥人的股份。

7.3 Termination for cause (misconduct, breach, misuse of funds):
因故終止（不當行為、違約、濫用資金）：
All phantom equity and formally transferred shares may be clawed back at the Founder's discretion and subject to legal proceedings if applicable.
所有虛擬股權及正式轉讓股份可由創辦人酌情追回，如適用可提起法律程序。

7.4 Upon termination, the Partner shall return all company property, devices, and confidential materials within 7 calendar days.
終止後，合夥人須於7個日曆天內歸還所有公司財產、設備及機密資料。

9. INTERNATIONAL REFERENCE PROVISIONS / 國際參考條款

(The following clauses are drawn from common international startup partnership best practices)

9.1 Good Leaver / Bad Leaver Distinction (UK/US Standard)
好離職者／壞離職者區分（英國／美國標準）：
A "Good Leaver" (resignation due to health, family emergency, or mutual agreement) retains vested shares at full value. A "Bad Leaver" (dismissal for cause, breach, or joining a competitor) forfeits all unvested equity and vested shares are subject to buyback at par value.
「好離職者」（因健康、家庭緊急情況或雙方協議而辭職）以全額保留已歸屬股份。「壞離職者」（因故被解僱、違約或加入競爭對手）喪失所有未歸屬股權，已歸屬股份須以面值回購。

9.2 Drag-Along Right (US/EU Standard)
強制隨售權（美國／歐盟標準）：
If the Founder receives a bona fide acquisition offer for 100% of SIMPLEX-ITY/5SENSESBEAUTY LIMITED, the Partner agrees to sell their shares on the same terms and conditions as the Founder.
若創辦人收到收購SIMPLEX-ITY/5SENSESBEAUTY LIMITED 100%股權的真誠收購要約，合夥人同意按與創辦人相同的條款及條件出售其股份。

9.3 Tag-Along Right (US/EU Standard)
隨售權（美國／歐盟標準）：
If the Founder sells their majority shareholding to a third party, the Partner has the right to sell their shares under the same terms.
若創辦人向第三方出售其多數股權，合夥人有權在相同條款下出售其股份。

9.4 Anti-Dilution Protection (US Standard)
防稀釋保護（美國標準）：
In the event of future equity rounds or new share issuances, the Partner's percentage shall not be diluted below 5% without the Partner's written consent, provided all vesting conditions have been met.
在未來股權輪次或新股發行的情況下，在所有歸屬條件均已達成的前提下，未經合夥人書面同意，合夥人的股份比例不得被稀釋至5%以下。

9.5 IP Assignment Clause (Standard International)
知識產權轉讓條款（國際標準）：
Any intellectual property, technology, tools, processes, or systems created by the Partner in the course of their role at SIMPLEX-ITY shall be owned solely by 5SENSESBEAUTY LIMITED.
合夥人在SIMPLEX-ITY職責範圍內創造的任何知識產權、技術、工具、流程或系統，均歸5SENSESBEAUTY LIMITED單獨所有。
