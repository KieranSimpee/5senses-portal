SIMPLEX-ITY REPORT MATRIX — DRAFT v1
Prepared by Simpee | 16 Jun 2026
=====================================

REPORT TYPE 1: BRAND PERFORMANCE REPORT
Audience: Brand owner / marketing manager
Their core question: "Is this platform worth my investment?"
Tone: Professional, data-driven, results-focused

SECTION A — Results Summary (First thing they see)
- Reporting Period
- Total GMV (USD)
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value
- Platform Commission Paid
- Net Revenue to Brand (estimated)

SECTION B — Influencer Performance
- Number of Influencers Active
- Top Performing Influencer (name + GMV)
- Total Lives Done
- Total Blog/Content Posts Done
- Average Influencer Reliability Score

SECTION C — Audience & Quality
- US Audience % (TikTok Shop primary market)
- Top Age Group of Buyers
- Engagement Rate %
- Refund Rate % (lower = better product-market fit)

SECTION D — Platform Value (Upsell angle)
- Brand Satisfaction Score (1-10)
- Month-on-Month Growth %
- Recommendation / Next Steps from Simplex-ity

SECTION E — Upgrade Prompt (for Basic tier brands)
- What data they are MISSING by not upgrading
- Cost of next tier vs potential GMV gain

=====================================

REPORT TYPE 2: INFLUENCER PERFORMANCE REPORT
Audience: Brand owner (to evaluate influencers)
         OR Influencer themselves (to see their own score)
Their core question (Brand): "Which influencers are worth keeping?"
Their core question (Influencer): "How am I performing? What's my score?"
Tone: Clear, motivating, transparent

SECTION A — Influencer Overview
- Influencer Name + Platform
- Reporting Period
- Reliability Score (1-10)
- AI Certified Status (Yes/No)

SECTION B — Deliverables
- Lives Agreed vs Lives Done
- Blogs/Content Agreed vs Done
- Completion Rate %

SECTION C — Sales Impact
- GMV Generated (USD)
- Commission Earned
- Avg GMV per Live
- Top Product Sold

SECTION D — Audience Quality
- Estimated Reach per Live
- Engagement Rate %
- Follower Count (platform)

SECTION E — Brand Feedback
- Brand Satisfaction with this Influencer
- Notes / Comments from Brand
- Recommended action (Continue / Upgrade / Review)
