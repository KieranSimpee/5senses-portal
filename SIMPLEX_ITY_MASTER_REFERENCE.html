01

Company Mission & Architecture

Simplify to Amplify — The Foundation

Who We Are

Simplex-ity is the world's first premier interactive e-commerce ecosystem built exclusively for the modern Asian beauty sector (K-Beauty, J-Beauty, C-Beauty).

We unite Brands, Influencers, and Consumers by embedding precise augmented reality (AR) virtual try-ons directly inside friction-free social streams and interactive, shoppable video environments.

Mission

To remove all transactional and technical friction from the discovery, sampling, and purchasing phases of Asian cosmetics — Simplifying the user journey to Amplify global brand sales.

Three Communities

Brands

Asian beauty suppliers accessing North American consumers via AI-powered campaigns and creator networks.

Influencers

Certified creators with personal Mini-Shops, earning commission ladders based on performance.

Consumers

Beauty shoppers with AR try-on, loyalty points, and seamless one-click checkout.

Platform Technology

TINT AR Integration

Real-time face-tracking and hyper-accurate cosmetic color rendering via global AR leader TINT.

TRY TOGETHER Live

Interactive live streams with real-time virtual try-on spots for consumers during broadcast.

Picture-in-Picture Checkout

Consumers checkout without leaving the live stream — video stays active during purchase.

Locked Operating Rules

Non-Negotiable Platform Standards

🔒 15% Platform Commission
Charged on ALL sales. Either on top of campaign package OR extracted as e-commerce transaction fee. Non-negotiable.
🔒 30% Discount — Stage-Gated
Only available for New Product Launch Campaigns. Brand absorbs cost from their marketing budget. Never platform-funded.
🔒 Free Gifting Rule
Brands must ship full-sized products to influencers before every campaign. No samples. No exceptions.
🔒 Reverse Exclusivity
Brands cannot hire direct competitor influencers during an active campaign block. Creator's conversion pool is protected.
🔒 Return Policy
Only unopened, hygiene-sealed items within 14 days. "Wrong shade" returns barred — TINT AI try-on eliminates this risk.
🔒 Legal Shielding
Brand partners assume 100% liability for product safety, FDA compliance, and packaging accuracy. Simplex-ity is the platform bridge only.
