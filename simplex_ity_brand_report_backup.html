Customer Insights 客戶洞察
What the Market is Telling Us
市場告訴我們的
🌙
Evening Skincare Routines Dominate
夜間護膚程序主導消費
68% of skincare purchases are evening-focused products. Cleansing oils, toners, and treatment serums see the highest repeat purchase rate at 3.2x per customer annually.
68%的護膚品購買集中在晚間護理產品。潔顏油、爽膚水和精華液的重複購買率最高，每位客戶每年平均購買3.2次。
68%護膚品購買集中夜間。潔顏油、爽膚水重複購買率最高。
👩
25–35 Age Group Drives Volume
25–35歲客群驅動主要銷量
The 25–35 demographic accounts for 58% of total orders. They favour mid-range Korean brands with verified creator endorsements. Average basket size $172 — highest of any segment.
25–35歲人口佔總訂單的58%。他們偏好有創作者認證的中高端韓國品牌，平均購物籃金額為172美元，是所有客群中最高的。
25–35歲佔58%訂單。偏好韓國品牌，平均客單價$172最高。
📱
Creator Content Drives 74% of Sales
創作者內容帶動74%銷售
Products featured by certified creators see 3.8x higher conversion versus standard listings. TikTok live sessions generate an average of $2,200 GMV per session — our strongest channel.
認證創作者推廣的產品轉化率是普通列表的3.8倍。TikTok直播每場平均產生2,200美元GMV，是我們最強大的渠道。
認證創作者推廣轉化率3.8倍更高。TikTok直播每場平均$2,200 GMV。
🌏
US Market Shows Strong Growth
美國市場展示強勁增長
US-based customers now represent 42% of total revenue, up from 31% in March. Korean beauty (K-beauty) discovery is accelerating, particularly in the 18–28 demographic on TikTok and Instagram.
美國客戶現佔總收入42%，高於3月份的31%。韓國美妝發現速度加快，尤其是在TikTok和Instagram上的18–28歲人口中。
美國客戶佔42%收入，從3月份31%上升。K-beauty在TikTok增速加快。
